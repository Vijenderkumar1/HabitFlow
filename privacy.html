HabitFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data Collection & Privacy

HabitFlow respects your privacy. All your habits, study logs, mood entries, water intake, weight records, and daily notes are stored locally on your device or in your private Google account space when logged in.

2. Google Sign-In & Authentication

If you choose to log in with your Google Account, we only use your Google profile name, email address, and avatar image to personalize your dashboard and isolate your saved habit records under your account ID.

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information or habit data with any third parties.

4. Your Controls

You can export your data anytime via CSV or JSON, reset your logs, or delete your account data locally at any time.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, feel free to visit HabitFlow GitHub Repository.
